Cruelco's History

Cruelco was founded in 1982 by the evil Doctor Blacksworth. After losing his reputation from his defeat by Calorieman, Doctor Blacksworth decided to take a backseat for future supervillains and founded Cruelco, the world's most successful international blackmarket. Doctor Blacksworth ran his business until 2001 when he went into hiding after Cruelco was discovered by the hero Pultryguy. Cruelco has since been exchanging hands from villain to villain, an almost guranteed source of profit and mayhem.
